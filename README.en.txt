Control and monitor your DAB H2D water pumps directly from Homey.

This app adds support for DAB pumps connected through the DAB H2D platform, including Esybox systems.

Features:
• Monitor pressure, flow, power and temperature
• Detect pump activity and water flow
• Receive alerts when the pump goes offline or reports an alarm
• Control PowerShower mode for increased pressure
• Enable Sleep mode for reduced pressure during quiet hours
• Use Homey Flow to automate your DAB pump

Supported devices:
• DAB Esybox (tested)
• Other DAB H2D-compatible pumps (limited support)

Need support or want to add your device?
Use the built-in diagnostic report Flow card to share device information.

This is an unofficial integration and is not affiliated with DAB.
